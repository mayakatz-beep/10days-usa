🗺️ How to Play

🎯
Goal: Build a valid 10-day road trip across the USA — fill all 10 slots with a connected route.
🔄
Your turn: Draw a tile from the deck or a discard pile, then place it in your journey or discard it.
🚶
On foot: Two adjacent (bordering) states can sit next to each other directly.
🚗
Car: Place between two states that are almost adjacent (one state separates them).
✈️
Airplane: Place between two states of the same color. Alaska & Hawaii count as any color.
📍
Pin a state: Tap the 📍 icon on a tile to highlight it on the map with its neighbors.
🏆
Win: When your route is valid, tap 🔍 Check to verify, then 🎉 Declare!
